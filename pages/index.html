Navigate the Journey

This web was conceived with a dual objective in mind: on one hand, it was meant to be a place where I can share my passions, both personal and professional ones, with the level of detail and complexity I consider appropiate. Moreover, I wanted that place to feel like my own, so developing my own website sounded like the perfect match. On the other hand, this web is meant to act like a beacon for those who also share my interests, and to tend a bridge towards collaboration, whereas that is having a debate, sharing information or even starting together an exciting project. I encourage every reader to feel free on getting in contact with me.

Throghout this site, you can share my journey through life, as it won't be a static object. The idea is to expand and evolve its content, and I'll try my best to keep it alive and updated. Getting into matter, initially there are three main sections:

  1. Projects: ended and in-progress projects, categorized based on tags corresponding to the subjects they're based on.
  2. Hobbies: describes activities I really enjoy and were I spent most part of my free-time.
    • 3D Printing
    • Sports
  3. Insights: blog-like space to discuss/share topics I found interesting or related to projects I'm working on.
